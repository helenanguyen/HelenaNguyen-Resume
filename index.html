Throughout my academic career, I was involved in a lot of volunteer work. It makes me feel fulfilling when I’m able to give back to my community. I also love to get involved with my culture. As I was born and raised in Vietnamese for 13 years, Vietnamese culture was an important part of my identity. I was a part of VSA (Vietnames Club) in my school and VAHSA (Vietnames American High School Alliance). Throughout four years in VSA, I have learned to embrace my culture and show other people the culture that I grew up with. I also met people who shared the same cultural admiration as me and we get to connect with each other through shared interests. Besides from my culture, I also admired other cultures around the world. I’m always thrilled to learn the diversity of different cultures compared to the culture that I’m familiar with. Culture is an important aspect that brings people together that we all should cherish.
